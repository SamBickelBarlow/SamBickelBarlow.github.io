Section 8: Big Data. Extracting a story from millions of prices (CC8)

Using Richard Davies' UK price data, I charted median monthly costs for care services, revealing a sharp April 2022 spike. This coincided with the Nationality and Border Act, restricting immigration, and the Russia-Ukraine war's price shock. Further econometric analysis is needed to identify the primary driver of rising care costs.

Sources: Richard Davies UK research database
