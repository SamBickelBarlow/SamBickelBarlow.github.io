Which sectors of economies are the biggest contributors to water stress?

Chart 3 which sectors contribute most to water stress. In most regions, with the exception of North America and Europe, agricultural water use dominates freshwater stress contribution.
While agriculture is naturally a water intensive economic activity, focusing on increasing efficient use of water in this sector could may pay the biggest dividends for policymakers interested in mitigating water shortages.

Source: United Nations Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) AQUASTAT
